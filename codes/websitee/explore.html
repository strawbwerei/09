St. John the Baptist Parish

One of the oldest churches in Taytay, known for its historical and religious significance.

Ita's Epalog

A well-known local food spot famous for its traditional flavors.

Kalayaan Park

Kalayaan Park in Taytay, Rizal, is a historic landmark that honors Filipino heroes and offers a serene environment for relaxation. It also serves as a community hub for cultural events, making it a great spot for both learning and leisure.

Taytay Tiangge

One of the largest garment markets in the country, making Taytay the "Garments Capital of the Philippines. A bustling marketplace where you can find affordable garments and trendy fashion pieces.

Dolores Chapel

A beautiful Catholic church that serves as a spiritual center for the community .A perfect place for architecture lovers and those interested in local Catholic traditions.

Club Manila East

Club Manila East in Taytay, Rizal, is a popular resort known for its exciting water attractions, including wave pools, slides, and kayaking lagoons, making it perfect for family getaways and adventure seekers.
